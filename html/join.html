Selection Process

If you are interested in joining our team as a Web Application Developer, here's an explanation of our selection process:


  1. Application: Please complete the Application Form. Be sure to highlight your relevant experience and why you would like to be a part of Nakama.
  2. Application Review: We will review all received applications and select candidates who best fit our requirements and core values.
  3. Initial Interview: Pre-selected candidates will be contacted for an initial interview, either in person or virtually, where we will discuss your experience, skills, and motivations in detail.
  4. Technical Evaluation: Candidates who advance in the process will participate in a technical assessment to demonstrate their skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other aspects relevant to the position.
  5. Finalists will be invited to a final interview with key members of the Nakama team, where we will discuss specific aspects of the role and assess cultural fit.
  6. Job Offer: Once the selection process is complete, we will select the most suitable candidate and extend a formal job offer.

We look forward to receiving your application and getting to know you soon!
