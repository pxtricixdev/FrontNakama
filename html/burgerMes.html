Burger of the Month

Sakura

Inspired by the constant pursuit of excellence and innovation in our cuisine. We wanted to offer our customers a unique experience that captures the essence of spring and celebrates Japan's rich culinary heritage.

Sakura, our spring-inspired burger, blends premium wagyu beef with cheese and red onion, offering a taste of Japan's culinary heritage.
Perfect for food enthusiasts seeking a unique gastronomic experience.

Our team of expert chefs works tirelessly to design the perfect combination of flavors, fresh ingredients and culinary creativity, all encapsulated in our Burger of the Month.


Each edition of our Burger of the Month is a dining experience you won't want to miss. From reinvented classics to bold innovations, our monthly selection is designed to satisfy every taste and craving. What can you expect from our Burger of the Month? Surprise! Excitement and an explosion of flavor in every bite!
