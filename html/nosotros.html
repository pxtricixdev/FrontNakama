OUR HISTORY

Discover Nakama, your vibrant and cozy corner in the heart of the city. Founded by Alex and Patricia, this place was born out of our passion for travel and food, inspired by an eye-opening journey through Asia. "Nakama" means "companions" in Japanese, a name that reflects our community spirit and our mission to share rich Asian cultures.

Our décor combines Asian elements with modern touches, creating the ideal atmosphere to savor unique and authentic burgers. Each burger is a culinary work of art, created with fresh, authentic ingredients that tell stories of faraway places.

Come to Nakama for good food in good company and an unforgettable culinary adventure.

WHICH SECTIONS?

At Nakama, every burger starts with high quality ingredients and a passion for exceptional flavors. We pride ourselves on serving culinary experiences that delight the senses and fulfill the taste for authenticity.

We highlight our wagyu beef burgers, renowned for their tenderness and intense flavor. Each variety is designed to enhance the unique qualities of this prestigious meat.

Our menu also includes everything from classic burgers to Asian-inspired innovations. Combine traditional techniques with modern touches to enjoy a classic dish redefined. Choose from our carefully crafted options and experience the quality and care we put into every burger.

FUTURE PLANS

At Nakama, we are excited to expand our passion for quality burgers nationwide. With the support of our community, we plan to open new locations in key cities while maintaining the high quality and welcoming atmosphere for which we are known.

We are committed to preserving the authenticity of our flavors and excellence in service at each new Nakama, creating meeting points for friends and families to enjoy special moments together.
